Open Institute is all about freedom. The freedom to research & create. It empowers not only the site builder, but also the client's editors, publishers and site manager.

Open Institute is fully layout-editable with a high usability drag & drop customization.

Open Institute has a simple, easy to use, powerful listing tool, which helps you to create a full range of filtered and sorted lists (including options for a re-finder form and layout choices) in a snap, and to place them on your page.

Open Institute is channel based. A channel is a contextual division, easily created by the site manager, which holds usability permissions and extra properties like: a main image, links and a contextual color. Each channel contains a lobby page (home page) or pages and content nodes from a various range of types like: Article, Publication, Event, etc. The lobby pages are layout grids easily built from various components like: lists, tables, calendars, images and images gallery.
